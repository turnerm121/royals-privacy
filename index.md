# Privacy Policy for Royals

**Last Updated: May 23, 2026**

Royals ("the App") is developed by Michael Turner. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

- Your chosen player name
- Game settings (sound, speed)
- Game statistics (wins, rolls, etc.)
- In-progress game state

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party. You can remove all of it at any time by deleting the App or by tapping "Reset Stats" in the Extras menu.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or networking services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Any changes to this policy will be posted at this URL.
