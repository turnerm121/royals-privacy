## Reporting a Bug

When reporting a bug, please include:

- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
- Your iOS version
- The app version (shown in the App Store)
- A description of the issue and the steps to reproduce it
- A screenshot or screen recording if possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I reset my stats?**
Open the app, tap **Extras** from the main menu, scroll to the **Stats** section, and tap **Reset Stats**.

**How do I change my player name?**
Open the app, tap **Extras**, type your new name in the **Player Name** field, and tap **Save Name**.

**How do I change game speed or turn off sound?**
Open **Extras** from the main menu — both options are available there.

**Does Royals collect any personal data?**
No. See the [Privacy Policy](../) for details.
